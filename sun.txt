PROJECT COMPLETE — RESUME BULLET (copy this):

  Trained a PPO agent from raw pixels to drive in OpenAI
  CarRacing-v2, implementing CNN feature extraction, GAE
  advantage estimation, and Gaussian policy from scratch
  in PyTorch. Achieved median eval reward of 864.7 over
  50 episodes (target: 700) with 66% of episodes scoring
  above threshold. Trained over ~5M steps on AWS EC2
  g4dn.xlarge with W&B experiment tracking.

INTERVIEW TALKING POINTS:
1. Why PPO over DQN? — Continuous action space (steering = float, not discrete)
2. What does GAE do? — Bias-variance tradeoff between TD and Monte Carlo
3. Why frame stacking? — Single frame has no velocity info, breaks Markov property
4. What does entropy bonus prevent? — Premature policy collapse / local optima
5. Why 8 parallel envs? — Decorrelates samples for stable gradient estimates
6. Hardest bug? — box2d SWIG/Python 3.13 float32 incompatibility; fixed with Float64Action wrapper
7. Why did training plateau then jump? — Exploration phase, then policy found "stay on track" reward signal
8. Result? — Median 864.7/1000, mean 632 (high variance from occasional crashes on hard tracks)
